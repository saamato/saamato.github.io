I'm a product designer living in New Jersey. I love coffee, pizza, hiking, typography, and my dog Finn. Here’s a little more about me.

I grew up in New Jersey. I went to Indiana University and majored in journalism. After college, I lived in New Jersey, Indiana, New Jersey (again), California, Arizona, Illinois, and Colorado. Now I'm back on the east coast.


I went to school to be a journalist, but somewhere along the way I discovered design. After about a decade in editorial design, I made the transition to UI/UX/Product design.


When I'm not designing, you can probably find me eating, reading, watching TV, and/or somewhere outdoors.
